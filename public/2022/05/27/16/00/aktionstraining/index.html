in english

Climate Camp Whether on demonstrations, small group or mass action, good preparation is important so that everyone can go into action safely! Among other things, we want to talk about must haves and no gos, as well as go through some possible situations practically. How do eviction situations go and how do we behave during arrests? Why do we say “Don’t talk to cops” and what does that have to do with anti-racism? We look forward to seeing you!
